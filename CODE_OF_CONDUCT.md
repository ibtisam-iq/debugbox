# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in DebugBox a harassment-free experience for everyone.

We commit to creating an open, inclusive, and respectful environment for all contributors.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Respectful and constructive communication  
- Being open to feedback  
- Accepting responsibility for mistakes  
- Focusing on what is best for the community  

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, insulting comments  
- Public or private harassment  
- Publishing others' private information  
- Discriminatory remarks  

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards and taking corrective action when unacceptable behavior occurs.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, GitHub discussions, PRs, issues, and private communication related to DebugBox.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or unacceptable behavior may be reported to:

**contact@ibtisam-iq.com**

All complaints will be reviewed fairly.
